Examination Guidlines for Students

  1. Reporting & Entry Candidates must report to the examination centre at least 15–30 minutes before the commencement of the examination><
  2. Candidates must carryAdmit Card / Hall Ticket
  3. Prohibited Items Candidates are strictly prohibited from carrying: Mobile phones, smart devices, calculators, or any electronic gadgets Notes, books, or any unauthorized materials Possession of such items may lead to cancellation of candidature or disqualification.
  4. Examination Rules Candidates are not allowed to leave the examination hall before the completion of the exam. No candidate will be permitted to enter the hall after the specified time. Any form of malpractice or unfair means will result in strict disciplinary action.
  5. Any candidate found violating rules, using unfair means, or not meeting eligibility criteria will have their candidature cancelled at any stage of the examination process
